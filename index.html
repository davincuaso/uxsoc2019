Tech and Beyond

this November 16 @ The Loft Innovation Wing, Arete

Immerse in a one-day experience of passion and creativity in this year’s third UX&Chill production, featuring various workshops and inspiring talks from some of the nation’s finest UX experts! Join us as we come together and fulfill our advocacy by doing what we love to do the most – innovation, design-thinking, and user experience. Spark your passion. Chill with UX.

Keynote Speaker

Ely Apao

Ely Apao is a UX professional past and present. He previously worked as a Product Development Manager in OLX and US Auto Parts. Now, he is a Customer Experience Manager at one of the largest Philippine conglomerates, JG Summit Holdings.

His zeal for UX can be seen even outside of his career. He founded UX Philippines, the non-profit organization that not only hosts the Philippines' largest community of individuals hooked on User Experience, but also raises awareness about design practice standards all over the Philippines.

Designing for Tech

Oliver Bayani

Oliver Bayani oversees User Research and UX writing at Home Credit Philippines where he designs digital products used by over 4 million Filipinos.

He was also a journalist for 8 years, writing about the most creative people in advertising for Marketing Magazine Philippines and Adobo Magazine. Before Home Credit, he was a Co-founder and Vice President of Product & Design in SnippetMedia, a news app enjoyed by over 2 million Filipinos.

Designing for Non-Tech

Wayne Yeang

Co-founder of the immersive entertainment company Tipsy Tales, Wayne Yeang knows UX isn’t just a tool for tech. He uses his extensive skill sets in market research and product development to create unique experiences that allow people to have unforgettable moments with the people they love. Wayne has a background in engineering and years of experience working with top companies across the globe with the Boston Consulting Group.

Workshop

Figma Philippines

In our workshop, you’ll master the basics of FIGMA with workshop leads from Figma Philippines. You’ll definitely be in good hands with UX designer and front-end web developer Chi Señires as well as design educator and product design manager Anj Garong.

Chi Señires is a Lead Product Designer at Cignal TV and a front-end web developer with over 4 years of experience in the industry. Before graduating from Ateneo de Manila University, she co-founded User Experience Society, the first global Filipino student organization that advocates and practices human-centered design in solving problems in the community. She also spends her time freelancing and volunteering in various creative communities like UXPH and Figma PH.

Anj Garong a design practitioner and educator with foundation in software engineering and years of practical knowledge from developing web and mobile apps, designing experiences, to managing teams at startups. She is immersed both in the academe and recently, in design communities, teaching Human-Centered Design at iACADEMY, and helping out Figma PH and UXSEA. Today, she spends most of her time in Sprout Solutions, a SaaS HR and Payroll technology startup, where she functions as a Product Design Manager, and she squeezes in workouts, movies, worship nights, and binge-sleeping for self-care.

Workshop

Principle Prototyping

In our workshop, you’ll master the basics of interactive prototyping using Principle. You’ll definitely be in good hands with Make Technology's "Prince of Principle", Jolo Desiderio.

Jolo Desiderio is a BFA Industrial and Product Design graduate from the University of the Philippines. Currently he is a User Experience Designer in Make Technology Incorporated.

Before his current role, he worked as a Product Designer for Maria Health making online products to meet business needs and user goals. He also worked part-time as a graphic designer for COL Financial Group.

Designing for Non-Tech

Errol Magdato

Errol Magdato is a seasoned Marketing professional with 15 years of experience in the field of Brand Management. He has worked passionately on growing the KFC brand in the Philippines and has been instrumental in delivering continuous sales growth for the past 15 years.

He is also an accomplished entrepreneur, opening his own business, Mystery Manila, the pioneer live role-playing game concept in the country. It has been featured in various TV programs, print publications and blogs, and now has 4 branches in the country and counting.

With the success of Mystery Manila, the company has branched out into a full-fledged agency specializing in unique engagement activities (from consumer activations, brand launches and corporate team-buildings) for various brands and clients.
